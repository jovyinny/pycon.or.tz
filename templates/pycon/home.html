PYCON TANZANIA.

Join us for the 5th Python Conference at the University of Dar es Salaam, Mlimani City Campus.. where Python developers, enthusiasts and learners from across East Africa will gather to share knowledge, build connections and celebrate out growing tech community.

Our Programme

What to expect at the 4th Annual Python Conference 2024

Workshops

Workshops are interactive sessions where you can learn new skills, get hands-on experience with Python, and even build something together. These sessions are designed to be interactive, engaging, and fun for everyone.

Talks

Lightning talks are short presentations, limited to 5 minutes, on any topic that might interest the Python community. It doesn't have to be something you've created, feel free to share something you've learned or a technique you think others will find useful.

Main Conference

PyCon is a place for developers from across the country (and beyond) to get together and celebrate Python. But what good is a PyCon without getting your hands dirty writing some code? .
