Why Sponsor PyCon Tanzania

Pycon Tanzania is run by volunteers on a non-profit basis. All the money we receive will go towards covering the costs of the event and reducing the cost for our attende

This includes:
  • Catering and refreshments
  • Venue rent
  • Technical services
  • Financial assistance for accommodation and travel for attendees and speakers on a low budget
  • Video recordings for talks
  • Conference merchandise and program booklet
  • Basic running expenses

Anything left over will be used for supporting future non-profit events.

Join us as we make history with the 5th Python Developer Conference in Tanzania! Be a part of a thriving community of developers and position your brand at the forefront of the rapidly growing technology sector in Africa. With an expected 200 attendees from both the local and international community, sponsoring this landmark event is a unique opportunity to connect with and reach a highly engaged audience. If you are interested in becoming a sponsor or if you have any questions, then please email us at sponsor@pycon.or.tz
